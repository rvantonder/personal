About Me

Hi! I'm Rijnard. I work a lot with software. I'm currently building autonomous agents from scratch, with focus on Formal Verification and rigorous proofs for software correctness.

I've worked remote at fast-moving startups since 2019. I really know how to build and deliver business impact. I'm lucky to have worked with strong teams at Google, Facebook, Sourcegraph, and Mysten Labs. I'm available for software contracting, or full-time for the right client, where LLMs, software engineering, and program analysis meet---especially agentic systems and high-assurance software. Reach out if you have interesting work and need results.

I completed my Computer Science Ph.D. at Carnegie Mellon University in 2019 where I worked on developing new ways to analyze, manipulate, and fix code.
